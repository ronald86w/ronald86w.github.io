Dear readers,

Welcome to my pages!

After working in the Chinese game industry for 5 years, I decided to come to the US for further study. Though I've gained game development experience from my previous work, especially in multiplayer FPS games, I'm still looking forward to exploring more interesting stuff. I wish to become a game developer like Josef Fares, the director of A Way Out, and It takes two. The way he introduces gameplay and tells stories impressed me deeply. Detroit: Become Human is also great in using interactive technologies to create a game world, which is also my favorite.

As a gamer, I can't figure out how many days and nights I've spent on turn-based strategy games, such as Civ V and Total War. City building games like Railway Empire and Cities Skylines are also my favorites. Game is such a good carrier to bring various experiences into real life.

In life, I like reading history stories and detective novels. Ken Follett, Agatha Christie, and Keigo Higashino are my favorite writers. Also, I like riding and traveling. Making friends on the trips and sharing our adventure stories is the coffee in my life.

Hope you have a better understanding of me and feel free to contact me please! Looking forwards to sharing more with you!

Sincerely,
Ronald Wang
